Bio.

I am a PhD student starting on Jan. 2017, supervised by Dr. Renate A. Schmidt and Dr. Giles Reger . I am interested in applying automated reasoning techniques to solve theoretical/real-world problems in first-order logic. Currently he is interested in:
  • Automated Reasoning,
  • Resolution Decision Procedure,
  • Query Answering.
